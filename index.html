Welcome Home

A cozy restaurant located in the heart of Sofia's city center, situated in a charming old house.

The charming atmosphere, delicious food and our friendly staff will make you feel right at home. Hidden away in a courtyard on Tsar Shishman street, we've been serving homemade food with love for over 10 years.
